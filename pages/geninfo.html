Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I prepare for this event?

    A good place to start will be the Research section on this website.

  2. What is the dress code for this event?

    Western formals is the standard dress code for this event. However, Indian dresses are permitted in the Indian Youth Parliament and Joint Action Committee.
    For boys: Tie, formal shirts and formal pants. A jacket is recommended. Shoes should be formal, no sneakers, sandals or slippers are allowed(certain exceptions in JAC and IYP). You may not be allowed to attend committee if the dresscode is breached.
    For girls: Western Business attire. Skirts and dresses are permitted as long as they are of respectable length.

  3. Where is the registration money used?

    The fee paid by each delegate is used in providing breakfast and lunch on both days of conference. It also includes refreshments such as water bottles, juices and chocolates. The cost of stationery is covered by this fee as well.

  4. Can we use electronic devices during the conference?

    You may use electronic devices only during the unmoderated caucus. At all other times, please refrain from using them. The only exception to this rule is the Security Council, where by the discretion of the chair, you can use your laptops throughout.
